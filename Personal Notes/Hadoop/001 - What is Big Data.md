# What is Big Data?

Big Data refers to high-volume, high-velocity, and/or high-variety information assets that demand cost-effective, innovative forms of information processing.

## The 3 V's of Big Data

1. **Volume**: The sheer amount of data generated (Terabytes, Petabytes).
2. **Velocity**: The speed at which data is generated and processed (Streaming, Real-time).
3. **Variety**: The different types of data (Structured, Semi-structured, Unstructured).

> [!INFO] Other V's
> Sometimes other V's are added:
> - **Veracity**: Trustworthiness of data.
> - **Value**: Business value derived.
> - **Variability**: Inconsistency in data flows.
