# Disk Space Commands

## `df` (Disk Free)
Shows free space on mounted filesystems.
- `df -h` — human-readable (GB, MB).

## `du` (Disk Usage)
Shows space consumed by a directory tree.
- `du -sh /home` — summary, human-readable.
