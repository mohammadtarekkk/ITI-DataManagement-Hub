# Analytical SQL - Lab 1

## Description
This lab contains solutions for evaluating employee performance and sales data using advanced Analytical SQL concepts (Window Functions).

## Questions Addressed & Implemented Logic
- **Q1**: Evaluates team productivity for each department by calculating cumulative hours worked and revenue generated over time, then ranking employees by their revenue contribution.
- **Q3**: Identifies employees whose revenue falls below the average revenue for their department.
- **Q4**: Flags employees with inconsistent revenue (differing by more than 20% from their department's average).
- **Q5**: Ranks employees based on their efficiency (revenue generated per hour worked) within their department.
- **Q7**: Identifies overloaded employees (those working >20% above the department average).
- **Q8**: Finds the top contributor to each project based on revenue generated.
- **Q9**: Generates a regional sales performance report, measuring the adjusted net revenue contribution of each salesperson (accounting for returned sales and restructuring costs) and ranking them within their region.
